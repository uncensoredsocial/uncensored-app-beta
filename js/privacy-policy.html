1. Introduction

UncensoredSocial (“we”, “us”, “our”, or the “Service”) is a social media platform focused on free expression and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, what we do with it, and what we deliberately choose not to collect.

By using UncensoredSocial, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Data we do NOT collect

UncensoredSocial is intentionally designed to avoid tracking and invasive data collection. Unless required for basic operation or security, we do not collect or store:

  • IP addresses for tracking or profiling. We do not permanently log or associate your IP with your account or activity.
  • Precise location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, cell tower) or inferred location history.
  • Device fingerprints or advertising identifiers, such as unique device IDs, mobile ad IDs, or browser fingerprint profiles.
  • Third-party analytics or ad tracking data. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, ad SDKs, or similar tracking services.
  • Behavioral or interest-based profiles used to target you with ads or content outside the Service.
  • Contact lists or address books from your device.
  • Biometric identifiers, such as facial data or fingerprints.

We also do not buy, sell, or trade data from third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

3. Data we do collect

To make the Service function at all, we need to collect some limited information that you provide directly or that is generated by your use of the Service.

3.1 Account information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • A username (handle) and display name
  • An optional profile picture or banner image
  • Optional profile fields you choose to fill in (e.g., bio, links)

We use this information only to operate your account and show your profile to other users.

3.2 User-generated content

When you use UncensoredSocial, you may create or upload content, including:

  • Posts and comments
  • Images, videos, and other media you choose to upload
  • Public profile content

This content is stored so that it can be displayed within the Service. You can delete your posts, and we will remove them from the live Service (subject to caching and backups, as explained below).

3.3 Authentication & session data

To keep you logged in and secure your account, we may store:

  • Authentication tokens or session identifiers (for example in cookies or local storage)
  • Basic technical logs necessary to protect the service from spam, abuse, and attacks

These are used solely to operate and secure the Service and are not used to track you across other websites or apps.

4. Direct messages & encryption

UncensoredSocial offers direct messages (“DMs”) that are designed to be end-to-end encrypted. This means:

  • Message contents are encrypted on your device before being sent.
  • We do not have the decryption keys and cannot read the contents of your DMs.
  • Encrypted messages may be stored on our servers for delivery and syncing across your devices, but only in encrypted form.

While we take steps to design DMs as private as possible, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your devices and login credentials safe.

Public posts, replies, likes, and follows are not end-to-end encrypted; they are visible to other users and, in many cases, to anyone on the internet.

5. Cookies & local storage

We keep tracking to an absolute minimum. We may use:

  • Strictly necessary cookies or local storage that keep you logged in, remember basic settings, or protect the Service from abuse (for example, rate limiting).
  • Local storage in your browser or device to store session tokens and preferences. This data is stored on your device and is not used to track you across the web.

We do not use cookies or similar technologies for advertising, behavioral tracking, or cross-site analytics.

6. Data retention

We aim to keep your data only for as long as it is needed to operate the Service.

  • Account data (username, profile, settings) is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or irreversibly anonymize associated personal data within a reasonable period, subject to technical limitations and legal requirements.
  • Posts and public content are stored until you delete them or your account is removed.
  • Encrypted DMs are stored as long as needed to sync across your devices, unless you or the other participant delete them.
  • Technical logs used for security and abuse protection are kept for the shortest practical period and are not used for profiling or advertising.

We do not maintain long-term logs of IP addresses or browsing behavior for tracking purposes.

7. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

We may use third-party infrastructure providers (for example, hosting, databases, or object storage) to operate the Service. These providers may technically process encrypted or stored data on our behalf, but they are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

We may disclose information only when:

  • Required to comply with a valid legal request or court order, and even then we can only provide what we actually have access to; or
  • Necessary to investigate or prevent abuse, spam, or security incidents affecting the Service or its users.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Using HTTPS to encrypt data in transit
  • Storing certain data in encrypted form and limiting who can access backend systems
  • Using end-to-end encryption for DMs so we cannot read message contents
  • Applying rate limiting and other protections against abuse and automated attacks

However, no system is perfectly secure. You are responsible for choosing a strong password, keeping your login details confidential, and securing your own devices.

9. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights over your personal data. Regardless of location, we aim to offer the following controls:

  • Access – You can view your profile information and content associated with your account.
  • Correction – You can edit your profile information at any time from your account settings.
  • Deletion – You can delete posts and your account. Removing your account will remove or anonymize associated data that we control, subject to technical and legal constraints.
  • Portability – Where technically feasible, you may request an export of data tied to your account.
  • Consent withdrawal – If we rely on your consent for a specific feature, you may disable that feature or contact us to withdraw consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10. Children’s privacy

The Service is not intended for, and we do not knowingly allow registration by, children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

If we become aware that a child under the applicable minimum age has created an account or provided personal information, we will take reasonable steps to delete that information and close the account.

If you believe that a child has used the Service in violation of this policy, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Service, our practices, or applicable laws. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and may notify you in the app or by other means.

Your continued use of UncensoredSocial after any changes take effect will constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.
